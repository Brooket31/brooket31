Brooke Tenney

Pastry Chef

My name is Brooke Tenney, i am a full time student along side my personally owned pastry business. While i am the founder and the owner of this pastry business, it is family operated and functioning. i am currently employed at panera bread as a caterer for the past 3 and a half years where I have learned to manage large responsiblities and orders. In order to maintain my businesses success i have used the tools and learning opportunities from my past employments to optimize my ability to manage and provide an organized establishment.

As part of my pastry business we set up and or attend local events where we bring many different kinds of pastry to hand out free samples and sell full sized portions. Some of our most popular pastries include: new and intriguing cupcake flavors, tres leches cakes, tiramisu, and buttery, flakey croissants. We offer single sized portions along with full cakes and other larger sharing sizes for events like birthday parties. Not only do we provide these larger portions, we also often do catering orders as well in which we are able to make and deliver large orders and set up at any local event in a delicious and beautiful manner.

While this business is family oriented and family friendly to make sure everyone gets a sweet treat, we also offer "adult only" pastries that include an alcoholic twist imbedded into some of our pastry options. This portion of the menu was inspired for parties and catering to make any 21+ event a little more exciting and stimulating. We have a liquor licsense as well in order to safely and responsibly make and serve these one of a kind adult desserts with a valid id of course. You can even place an order for mixed pastries to have both regular and alcoholic options for both kids and adults so no one is left out. For those who have dietary restrictions, we can also make any dessert gluten free, vegan, vegetarian, and even sugaar free upon request with the same great tastes and flavors.
